How It Works

Built to find high-probability setups — automatically

The engine scans 8 timeframes simultaneously, grades every setup, and validates each trade against historical patterns before triggering.

8-Timeframe Scan

Higher timeframes set direction. Lower timeframes find the entry. The system waits for alignment across all 8 before acting — no chasing.

Ticker Intelligence

Every ticker gets a deep profile: how it reacts to setups, preferred pullback patterns, and volatility behavior. Ticker-specific rules, not generic playbooks.

Setup Grading

Every trade gets a grade: Prime, Confirmed, or Early. Prime setups get wider stops and more time. Early setups are managed tightly.

Self-Calibration

Every outcome feeds back. Win rates, entry paths, exit patterns — the system continuously recalibrates parameters to evolving market conditions.

AI Trade Review

Every setup is validated against historical patterns and market conditions before triggering. The AI reviews confidence, risk flags, and past outcomes — rejecting setups that don't meet the bar.

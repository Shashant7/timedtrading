# Batch 0 / Deliverable 7 — 3 YouTube Shorts scripts

> **Format:** vertical 9:16, ≤ 60 sec each.
> **Visual constant:** the Daily Brief screenshot stays full-frame for the entire Short. Founder voiceover only — no talking-head. Captions on-screen always (TikTok / Reels portability).
> **Voice:** off-site, "you" allowed in the hook. The instant a level / price is read, switch to *"the model says"* / *"the brief shows"*.
> **Disclaimer:** on-screen text last 3 seconds + first line of the description.
> **Numbers in `{{brackets}}`** = operator confirms the live morning's numbers before recording. The bracketed values below are templates the operator fills in for any given morning.
> **Suggested post time:** **9:05 AM ET**, weekdays. Five-minute lag after the brief drop maximizes pre-open dwell.

---

## Shared production notes

- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16. Vertical only. Don't reuse the long-form 16:9 capture.
- **Audio:** founder voice clean, no music bed under the levels. Optional subtle ambient under hook + outro only.
- **Captions:** burned-in (not auto-captioned). Three of these Shorts will be repurposed to TikTok / Reels where YouTube's auto-captions don't carry.
- **Hashtags (YouTube description):** `#stocks #swingtrading` (max two — per voice guide §6). No `#investingtips`.
- **Title field:** front-load the ticker — *"SPY today: the levels the model is watching"*, etc. Avoid click-bait formulations.
- **Pinned comment:** identical across all three — *"Daily Brief is free at timed-trading.com. Not investment advice."*

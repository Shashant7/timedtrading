| | **Founder (you)** | **Product (@TimedTrading)** |

| **Voice** | First person. Vulnerable, builder, “why I built this.” | Third person / “the system.” No opinions. |

## Cross-account strategy (how to grow both without spam)

### Default pattern (weekend / week-ahead posts)

1. **Founder posts first** (image + story + macro *context*, not buy calls).
2. **4–24 hours later**, Product **quote-tweets** the founder post with a **data-only** caption (no repeat of the personal story):

   > Week-ahead structure — SPY / QQQ / IWM on the Timed Trading bubble map (HTF × LTF). Full universe + live ledger: timed-trading.com/proof.html  
   > Not investment advice.

3. **Do not** have both accounts post identical copy the same hour—that reads as a bot farm.

### When to repost vs quote-tweet

| Action | Use when |
|--------|----------|
| **Quote-tweet** (preferred) | Product adds value: disclaimer, link, one-line structure summary |
| **Repost / RT** | Rare—only celebratory milestones (e.g. proof page shipped) |
| **Founder replies** on Product posts | Add human context in replies (“here’s why the map looked like this Saturday”) |

### Bio / pin wiring

- **Founder bio:** Builder of Timed Trading · discipline over discretion · link `proof.html` · “Structure posts → @TimedTrading”
- **Product bio:** Lifecycle signals (Setup → Trim → Exit) · live ledger · follow founder for the build story · link `proof.html`
- **Founder pin:** Best thread: origin story OR latest week-ahead prep (rotate monthly)
- **Product pin:** “What is a Trim?” explainer + screenshot of kanban, link proof

### Follow graph

- Founder **follows** Product; Product **follows** Founder (signals legitimacy).
- Founder occasionally @mentions Product in **second tweet** of a thread, not tweet 1.

---

## What Product should **never** post (until automation spec is built + 2 weeks manual QA)

- Hot takes, politics, competitor shade
- “You should buy/sell X”
- Charter / pricing urgency
- Founder-style vulnerability threads

---

## What Founder should **never** post

- Live trade alerts that Product hasn’t logged yet (timing mismatch kills trust)
- Backtest stats without window + “before fees” + link
- Price-drop panics (“only 3 charter seats left”)—conflicts with trust-first phase

| Day | Founder | Product |
|-----|---------|---------|
| **Sat–Sun** | Week-ahead prep + bubble map (see kickoff doc) | Quote-tweet founder OR rest |
| **Mon** | “What I’m watching this week” (macro only) | ISM week open: index structure screenshot |
| **Tue–Thu** | 1 reply-heavy day in trader Twitter | Trim/Exit receipt if any |
| **Fri** | Honest week reflection (win or loss) | Weekly ledger snippet → proof |

## Automation gate

Batch 0.5 (Discord HITL → X queue) stays **off** until:

- [ ] 15+ founder posts published manually
- [ ] 10+ product quote-tweets/receipts manually
- [ ] Operator comfortable with voice (reads aloud without cringe)

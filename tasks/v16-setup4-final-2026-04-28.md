## What Setup #4 does

Captures the "tight base near 52w high → breakout" pattern that
dominates bull-grind regimes where tt_pullback has nothing to fire on
(e.g. post-FOMC October 2025: SPY $658→$673 in 11 days, no real
pullbacks).

**LONG**: within 3% of 52w high AND breaking above prior-day high AND
tight base (<5% over last 5 bars) AND state allows AND rvol confirms.

**SHORT mirror**: within 3% of 52w low AND breaking below prior-day
low (same pattern flipped).

## Refinements applied

1. **Threshold loosened** 1.5% → 3% (Ripster's number was for ATH
   breakouts where price IS at the high; our universe trades 2-4%
   below ATH commonly during accumulation).
2. **Tight-base loosened** 3% → 5% (admits high-momentum names that
   don't compress to <3%).
3. **ETF cohort rvol 1.5x** vs single-stock 1.0x (broad-market ETFs
   need institutional flow confirmation; single stocks fire at
   normal volume).
4. **Follow-through required**: current price must exceed prior bar
   close — filters single-bar wick breakouts.

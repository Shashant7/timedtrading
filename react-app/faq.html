Scoring & Signals

Each ticker receives a composite score (0–100) computed from multiple timeframes (5-minute through monthly). The engine evaluates trend direction, momentum strength, mean-reversion signals, volume profiles, and risk/reward ratios. Higher scores indicate stronger trade setups. Scores update every minute during market hours.

Tickers flow through a Kanban-style pipeline based on their scoring trajectory:

  • Setup — Building a case. The ticker is showing early signs of a potential move.
  • Enter — Ready for entry. Score and conditions align for a new position.
  • Hold — Active position. The trade thesis remains intact.
  • Defend — Caution. The trade is under pressure; tighten stops or reduce exposure.
  • Trim — Take partial profits. Momentum is fading but the trend may continue.
  • Exit — Close the position. The trade thesis is invalidated.

The Active Trader dashboard arranges tickers into columns (lanes) by their current action phase. Each card shows the ticker symbol, score, direction (LONG/SHORT), price, and sparkline. Cards are sorted by score within each lane, so the strongest setups appear at the top. Click any card to open the detail view with full chart and indicator breakdowns.

The Daily Brief is a morning summary generated each trading day before the market opens. It highlights the top-scoring setups, key sector rotations, macro backdrop, and notable changes from the previous session. Think of it as your pre-market game plan.

## Iffy-tells checklist (extracted)

1. **Erratic deep retracements** — trend quality degrades before the crash print.
2. **Pullback overbalances in time** — corrective leg lasts longer than prior corrections.
3. **Undercuts last major swing low** — not a routine digression.
4. **Shakeout to new highs after that damage** — late-bull trap, not fresh confirmation.
5. **Bull → multi-year sideways morph** — regime change on the higher timeframes.
6. **Monthly MACD rollover negative** — HTF momentum confirms weaken.
7. **Distribution volume** — volume spikes preferentially on down days.
8. **Critical support just above 50% retracement** — lost zone = further damage.
9. **Do not hero** — wait for stabilization before new risk.
